ABOUT STUYESPORTS
The Stuyvesant Competitive Gaming Club (SCGC) is Stuyvesant's largest Gaming Community, providing a casual environment (the club) and a competitive environment (StuyEsports) for players of all skill level. For competitive play, we have teams participating in EzEsports, a city-wide esports league for League of Legends and Valorant.
